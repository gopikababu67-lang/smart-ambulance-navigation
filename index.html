CORE FEATURES

Intelligent systems working in tandem to minimize emergency response time and maximize patient survival rates.

🗺️
REAL-TIME ROUTE OPTIMIZATION
Dynamic path recalculation using live traffic feeds, accident data, and road closure reports updated every 30 seconds for the fastest route.
🚦
SIGNAL PREEMPTION SYSTEM
Automatically communicates with traffic signal controllers to turn signals green up to 300 meters ahead of the approaching ambulance.
📡

HOW IT WORKS

A seamless pipeline from emergency call to hospital arrival, powered by intelligent algorithms.

01
📞
EMERGENCY CALL
Patient call received at control center. Dispatcher logs incident type, severity, and location coordinates into the system.
02
🚑
UNIT DISPATCH
Nearest available ambulance identified using GPS fleet tracking. Optimal unit dispatched with pre-loaded route in under 30 seconds.
03
📡
CONGESTION ANALYSIS
AI engine fetches live traffic data, calculates 3 alternate routes, selects fastest path with real-time rerouting capability.
04
🚦
SIGNAL CLEARANCE
Connected traffic infrastructure receives preemption commands, turning all signals green along the ambulance's confirmed route.
05
🏥
HOSPITAL READY
Receiving hospital is pre-alerted with patient vitals, ETA, and resource needs. Emergency team assembled before arrival.
